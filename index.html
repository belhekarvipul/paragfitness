You get what you work for, not what you wish for!!

Parag Fitness Studio (PFS) was founded in the year 2003. It has been successfully operating for last 18 years. We have experienced and certified (K11 & REPS) trainers. It is a unisex gym located at Pune, India. We offer detailed personal training along with sharing of complete practical and theoretical knowledge. All our personal trainings are goal oriented and inclusive of diet counselling & follow up. We chalk but the workout program and the diet plan considering the complete medical history of the client. We also offer multiple types of general training package with basic guidance. The thing that differentiates our gym & service from others are: dedication, knowledge, technique, expertise. Services

We always look for multiple services under one roof. However, always think wisely before making the choice of gym you would like to go to. We offer all that is required for you to be fit.


Weight Training and Cardio

Both these are essential for the overall fitness of an individual. Our specifically designed programmes ensure that you absoultely maximize the performance.

Personal Training

Our certified and experienced trainers can help you through one to one sessions to make sure you achieve your goals witin stipulated time. We set realistics targets and give all the support that is required.

Recovery

Your rest and recovery is as important as your training. This is why we share the knowledge about mobility drills and post workout stretching exercise.

Nutrition

A lot of us underestimate the importance of nutretion! It plays the major role in your day-to-day life hence we provide you with a personally tailored meal plan with every membership.
